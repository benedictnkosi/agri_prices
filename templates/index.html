Features

Main Feature

This Hotel Manager App has loads of features that are designed for the South African market. We know exactly what you need.

Single View Calendar

Have a quick glance of all your reservations from Booking.com, Airbnb, your own website and other channels.

Free Direct Bookings Website

Pay less commission with direct bookings. Your guests can book and make payments directly on your website.

Manage Reservations

Add notes, payments, extra charges, guest ID and even add cleaning to the reservation. We help you remember the important stuff.

Automate Messages

Send scheduled emails to your direct booking guests. Now you can let them know about your load shedding schedule on arrival.

Manage Your Rooms

Update room names, change your prices, update the room description. You don't have to pay someone to update the small things.

Block A Room

Have a burst pipe or just not up to hosting today? block the room on the app in 5 seconds. You can also view and manage blocked rooms.

Occupancy Report

Do you know how many nights you were booked last month? We have that taken care of also, see occupancy per room. Know which rooms are not performing.

Invoicing

Now you don't have to use a different tool to create invoices. You can view an invoice for a reservation by a click of a mouse.
